Moldova - Getting to

Direct: London airports to Chișinău KIV airport

Air Moldova (Stansted) and Wizz Air (Luton) fly direct. Note that flights may not appear until 6mths prior to departure date. Flight time is around 3hrs 15min

One stop: London airports to Chișinău KIV airport

Austrian Air, Lufthansa, and LOT (all from Heathrow) offer flights with 1 stop and are a very good alternative. We would recommend at least a 1 hour stop over to ensure time to transfer and also for your checked in baggage (if applicable) to be moved over.

Getting around in Chișinău

Transport to and from the wedding venue will be provided. The coach ride to Purcari Winery will take roughly 2.5 hours. More details will come on this via email you RSVP with!
Chișinău is very centrally located with all the recommended bars, restaurants, and hotels located within walking distance of each other. If you would like to grab a taxi, we highly recommend booking one via the iTaxi orYandex ride-hailing apps.

City Park Hotel (4*)

4-star hotel in the heart of the city, two minutes walk from the main city park and the main street of Chisinau. Website

The convenient location makes it an ideal base for visiting attractions in the centre of Chișinău, or you can arrange a winery tour with the help of the concierge team. The hotel also offers transfer services to and from Chișinău International Airport. Website

Nobil Luxury Boutique Hotel (5*)

Five star Chișinău hotel, with 40 rooms, including Business, Ambassador and Presidential Suites. The hotel also features a panoramic restaurant on its rooftop terrace. In addition to this, the hotel also has a Spa Centre, a Fitness Centre, and a beauty salon. Website

Moldova - Things to do

Chișinău city centre walking tour

Chișinău is not particularly big so everything in the centre is within walking distance. For a nice tour of the centre we recommend walking along the Ștefan cel Mare (Stephen the Great) boulevard. Start from Piața Marii Adunări Naționale (Great National Assembly Square) and the Cathedral park and walk towards Stephen the Great's monument and the park behind it. Carry on via the National Opera & Ballet theatre and the presidency then go up Sfatul Țării Street. Turn left onto 31 August 1989 Street and walk along the back of the Ștefan cel Mare park. Walk past the National History & Art museums and the National Palace concert hall then turn right on Alexander Pushkin street and carry on until you hit Valea Morilor Park. The park is lovely and has a lake in the middle for you to finish your walk in. You will be very close to a lot of the restaurants we've recommended so take your pick!

Old Orhei Monastery

If you would like to venture out outside Chișinău, Orheiul Vechi (Old Orhei) has stunning views of the Răut River as well as a monastery going into the cliffs around the river bend.If you go around there you can also head down to the Butuceni Village, which is a wonderfully preserved traditional Moldovan Village. Vila Etnica (Ethnic Villa) serves a very good traditional lunch but make sure you book ahead.

Cricova Wine Tours

If you have time, we would highly recommend a Winery tour and tasting at either Cricova or Mileștii Mici. Both have massive underground cellars where you can go in by car / golf cart and the sheer amount of wine is not something you will see very often. Cricova is located 15km North of Chișinău. It also makes amazing champagne and offer tours of the cellars, wine production facilities and tastings in English. Trip Advisor

Mileștii Mici

Mileștii Mici have the biggest wine collection in the world and also 250km of underground cellars (of which 55km are in use for the public). It also features a wine fountain and an underground waterfall. We recommend renting a car if you are planning to go as you can essentially drive through the cellars (yes, they are that big). The tour guide will join the group in one of the cars and stop along the way.
